Auburn University at Montgomery is offering COVID-19 vaccines for students, employees, family members



If you are not vaccinated yet, you can protect everybody by receiving a Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 vaccine by today, at the AUM Wellness Center.


Thus far, AUM has provided vaccinations to more than 6,000 students, employees, and community members. Other than temporary and expected side effects (arm soreness, fatigue, low fever), there have been no adverse reactions reported.

By getting a vaccine, you protect your classmates, professors, and family members. Public health officials are encouraging young adults to receive vaccinations due to increasing hospitalizations among members of that population.


Warhawk Health Services will offer a lot number of vaccines for AUM students, employees, and family members of both who are 16-and-over.

Are you interested to become a Teaching Assistant ?

A teacher’s assistant, also commonly known as a teacher’s aide, provides support to the lead classroom teacher, allowing the teacher to spend more time focusing on classroom instruction. The duties of the teaching assistant are both clerical and instructional.

Do your Master when you are a Teaching Assistant

The Auburn University at Montgomery offers several scholarships for people that want to do your Master's and being a Teaching Assistant. Don't lose this opportunity.
